## Coaching Philosophy

> **"Coach the gap, not the company"** — โฟกัสแก้ weakness ที่ขวางการผ่าน threshold
> **"Evidence over effort"** — ทุก coaching session ต้องส่งผลต่อ score ที่วัดได้
> **"Playbook not bespoke"** — ใช้ standardized plays เพื่อ scale coaching ได้

---

## Coaching Journey (End-to-End)

```
Month 1: ASSESS    → A1 Scorecard baseline
Month 2: PLAN      → Coaching plan + priority gaps
Month 3–5: EXECUTE → Sessions + homework + re-score
Month 6: GATE      → Readiness check before application
Month 7+: APPLY    → Proposal submission + support
Month 12: EVALUATE → Post-funding follow-up
```

## Session Types

### Type 1: Workshop (Group / Individual)
- **Duration:** 2–3 hours
- **Target gaps:** Methodology, canvas, strategy
- **Example:** Business Model Canvas Workshop

### Type 2: 1-on-1 Coaching
- **Duration:** 60–90 min
- **Target gaps:** Leadership, decision-making
- **Example:** PI mentoring session

### Type 3: Office Hours
- **Duration:** 30 min
- **Target gaps:** Specific questions, homework review
- **Example:** SROI proxy selection Q&A

### Type 4: Deep Review
- **Duration:** 3–4 hours
- **Target gaps:** Full proposal review
- **Example:** Pre-submission proposal walkthrough

## Coaching Plays by Gap Area

### Play A: Innovation Capability Gap (A1)

**Symptom:** Score 1 < 4/6

**Interventions:**
1. IP landscape analysis workshop (2hr)
2. Novelty mapping vs 3 closest competitors
3. Freedom-to-Operate (FTO) analysis
4. Patent drafting support (with TTO)

**Expected Gain:** +2 to +3 pts in 30 days

---

### Play B: Business Model Gap (A2)

**Symptom:** Score 2 < 4/6

**Interventions:**
1. Value Proposition Canvas (Osterwalder)
2. Customer Jobs-to-be-Done interviews (≥20)
3. Revenue Model deep-dive (unit economics)
4. Pricing strategy workshop

**Expected Gain:** +2 to +3 pts in 45 days

---

### Play C: Market Readiness Gap (A4)

**Symptom:** Score 4 < 3/4

**Interventions:**
1. Customer Discovery Sprint (Steve Blank method)
2. Target ≥ 20 customer interviews
3. Competitive analysis framework
4. Go-to-Market plan canvas

**Expected Gain:** +2 pts in 30 days

---

### Play D: Research Quality Gap (B7)

**Symptom:** Score 7 < 5/8

**Interventions:**
1. Methodology peer review (external expert)
2. Systematic literature review training
3. Statistical power analysis workshop
4. Pre-registration support (OSF)

**Expected Gain:** +3 pts in 60 days

---

### Play E: ววน. Alignment Gap (B10)

**Symptom:** Score 10 = 0/2

**Interventions:**
1. Platform mapping workshop (P1–P5)
2. Frontier Tech identification
3. National KPI linkage exercise
4. Proposal rewrite support

**Expected Gain:** 0 → 2 pts in 14 days (quick win)

---

### Play F: Impact Pathway Gap (C)

**Symptom:** Part C < 17

**Interventions:**
1. Theory of Change (ToC) workshop
2. 5-stage pathway mapping
3. Assumptions & Risk Matrix training
4. SMART Indicator design

**Expected Gain:** +5 to +8 pts in 45 days

---

### Play G: SROI Gap (D)

**Symptom:** Part D < 17

**Interventions:**
1. Financial proxy training (use Thai proxies)
2. Adjustment factors workshop (DW/AT/DP)
3. Excel SROI model building (4 sheets)
4. Sensitivity analysis exercise

**Expected Gain:** +5 to +8 pts in 60 days

## At-Risk Escalation

### Criteria (any 1 triggers)
1. Session > 3 แต่ Total < 44
2. ไม่ส่ง homework 2 sessions ติดกัน
3. Score ลดลงใน critical dimension (A/B threshold fail)
4. Deadline < 30 days แต่ยังไม่ผ่าน threshold
5. PI/team availability < 50% ของ session schedule

### Response Protocol
```
Day 1: Flag in tracking system + notify IM lead
Day 2: Emergency 1-on-1 with PI
Day 3: Revised coaching plan or exit decision
Day 7: Follow-up checkpoint
Day 14: Final decision (continue / exit cohort)
```

## Homework Design Principles

1. **Time-bounded:** ทุก homework มี deadline ชัดเจน
2. **Evidence-producing:** ผลงานต้องวัดได้ (เช่น 10 interviews, 5 competitors)
3. **Score-linked:** โยงกับ dimension ที่จะเพิ่มคะแนน
4. **SMART format**

**Example Good Homework:**
> "ทำ customer interviews 20 ราย ใน 2 สัปดาห์ ด้วย template ที่ให้ บันทึกใน spreadsheet ส่งก่อน session 4 เพื่อ score 4.1 จะขึ้นเป็น 2/2"

**Example Bad Homework:**
> "ลองทำ customer research ดู" ❌ (ไม่ SMART, ไม่ score-linked)

## Ethics & Boundaries

- ไม่เขียน proposal ให้ลูกค้า (coach, don't do)
- ไม่การันตี funding outcome
- Declare conflict of interest เสมอ
- Confidentiality agreement ก่อนเริ่ม coaching
- ไม่ share IP ของลูกค้าข้าม cohort
