## Scoring Philosophy

> ให้คะแนนตาม **evidence-based criteria** ไม่ใช่ความรู้สึก
> ทุก score ต้องมี **justification 1-2 ประโยค** อ้างอิงหลักฐาน
> Default: ให้คะแนน **conservative** เมื่อหลักฐานไม่ชัด

---

## PART A — Standard IDE Assessment (28 pts)

### Dimension 1: Innovation Capability (6 pts)

#### 1.1 Novelty & Differentiation (3 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 3 | นวัตกรรมใหม่ระดับประเทศ/โลก + มี IP protection ชัดเจน |
| 2 | ปรับปรุงจากที่มีอยู่อย่างมีนัยสำคัญ + มีจุดต่างเทียบคู่แข่ง |
| 1 | คล้ายกับคู่แข่ง แต่มีจุดเด่นเฉพาะกลุ่ม |
| 0 | ไม่มีความแตกต่าง / me-too product |

#### 1.2 IP Strategy (2 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 2 | Patent/Trade secret + FTO analysis + licensing strategy |
| 1 | มี IP application หรือ trademark แต่ไม่ครบ |
| 0 | ไม่มี IP plan |

#### 1.3 Technical Feasibility (1 pt)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 1 | TRL ≥ 4 + proof of concept validated |
| 0 | TRL < 4 / ยังพิสูจน์ไม่ได้ |

---

### Dimension 2: Business Model (6 pts)

#### 2.1 Value Proposition (3 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 3 | แก้ pain point ที่ชัดเจน + quantified benefit + customer-validated |
| 2 | Value prop ชัด แต่ขาด quantification |
| 1 | มี value prop แต่ไม่โดดเด่น |
| 0 | ไม่ชัดเจน |

#### 2.2 Revenue Model & Customer Acquisition (2 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 2 | Multiple revenue streams + CAC/LTV validated |
| 1 | Single revenue stream + unit economics plausible |
| 0 | No revenue model |

#### 2.3 Market Size & Growth (1 pt)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 1 | TAM ≥ 1B THB + CAGR ≥ 10% + ref. source |
| 0 | Market < 1B / unclear |

---

### Dimension 3: Team & Organization (4 pts)

#### 3.1 Team Composition & Expertise (2 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 2 | Tech + Business + Domain คลุมครบ + track record |
| 1 | คลุม 2/3 ด้าน |
| 0 | ครอบคลุมเพียง 1 ด้าน |

#### 3.2 Management Capacity & Governance (2 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 2 | Board/Advisor + governance structure + accountability |
| 1 | Founder-led + informal governance |
| 0 | ไม่มีโครงสร้างการกำกับ |

---

### Dimension 4: Market Readiness (4 pts)

#### 4.1 Customer Discovery & Validation (2 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 2 | ≥ 20 customer interviews + LOI/pilot signed |
| 1 | 5–20 interviews + initial interest |
| 0 | < 5 interviews |

#### 4.2 Competitive Analysis (1 pt)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 1 | Competitor map + positioning + SWOT |
| 0 | Superficial / missing |

#### 4.3 Go-to-Market Strategy (1 pt)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 1 | Channel strategy + pricing + launch timeline |
| 0 | No GTM plan |

---

### Dimension 5: Financial Sustainability ⭐ (4 pts)

#### 5.1 Funding Strategy & Diversification (2 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 2 | Multiple funding sources + pipeline for next round |
| 1 | Single source + awareness of alternatives |
| 0 | Grant-dependent only |

#### 5.2 Financial Projections & Unit Economics (1 pt)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 1 | 3-year projection + gross margin + breakeven point |
| 0 | Optimistic/unclear projections |

#### 5.3 Resource Efficiency & Budget Allocation (1 pt)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 1 | Budget tied to milestones + burn rate discipline |
| 0 | Unclear allocation |

---

### Dimension 6: Network & Partnerships (4 pts)

#### 6.1 Partnership Quality & Commitment (2 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 2 | ≥ 2 signed MOU with industry/research + active collaboration |
| 1 | 1 partnership or LOIs only |
| 0 | No formal partnership |

#### 6.2 Ecosystem Integration (1 pt)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 1 | Part of innovation cluster / supply chain / accelerator |
| 0 | Isolated |

#### 6.3 Stakeholder Engagement Plan (1 pt)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 1 | Stakeholder map + engagement calendar |
| 0 | Ad-hoc engagement |

## PART B — Research-Specific (22 pts)

### Dimension 7: Research Quality (8 pts)

#### 7.1 Methodological Rigor (3 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 3 | Pre-registered + control group + statistical power analysis |
| 2 | Sound methodology + sample size justified |
| 1 | Basic methodology |
| 0 | Flawed / missing |

#### 7.2 Evidence Base & Literature (3 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 3 | ≥ 20 citations + systematic review |
| 2 | 10–20 citations + narrative review |
| 1 | < 10 citations |
| 0 | No literature review |

#### 7.3 Research Team Capability (2 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 2 | PI with Q1 publications + multi-disciplinary team |
| 1 | PI has relevant publications |
| 0 | No track record |

---

### Dimension 8: Technology Transfer (6 pts)

#### 8.1 TRL/MRL Advancement (3 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 3 | Current TRL ≥ 6, target TRL ≥ 8 |
| 2 | Current TRL 4–5, target TRL 6–7 |
| 1 | Current TRL 2–3 |
| 0 | TRL unclear |

#### 8.2 IP & Commercialization Strategy (2 pts)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 2 | Patent filed + licensing model + commercial partner |
| 1 | IP plan but no concrete steps |
| 0 | No commercialization path |

#### 8.3 Industry Collaboration (1 pt)
| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| 1 | Signed industry MOU with co-investment |
| 0 | No industry link |

---

### Dimension 9: Scalability & Replication (6 pts)

#### 9.1 Adaptability to Contexts (2 pts)
Evidence of ≥ 2 use cases / geographies tested.

#### 9.2 Replication Strategy & Knowledge Transfer (2 pts)
Playbook / SOP / training materials ready.

#### 9.3 Scaling Economics (2 pts)
Cost per unit decreases with scale (marginal cost analysis).

---

### Dimension 10: ววน. Alignment ⭐ (2 pts)

#### 10.1 Platform & Frontier Technology Alignment (1 pt)
- 1 ✅: ระบุ Platform P1–P5 + Frontier Tech + cite แผน ววน.
- 0 ❌: ไม่ระบุ / ไม่เชื่อมโยง

#### 10.2 National KPI Contribution (1 pt)
- 1 ✅: เชื่อมโยง OKR/KPI ชาติ + ตัวเลขเป้าหมาย
- 0 ❌: ไม่มี / ผิวเผิน

## PART C — Impact Pathway Logic (25 pts)

### C1: Pathway Architecture (7 pts)
- C1.1 Five-Stage Structure (3): Input→Activity→Output→Outcome→Impact ครบ
- C1.2 Stakeholder Mapping (2): ระบุ ≥ 3 groups + role
- C1.3 Temporal Logic (2): Short/Medium/Long-term outcomes

### C2: Causal Logic & ToC (8 pts)
- C2.1 If-Then Statements (3): logic chain ชัดเจนทุก step
- C2.2 Theoretical Grounding (3): อ้างอิง theory/framework
- C2.3 Evidence Base (2): cite เปรียบเทียบกับโครงการอื่น

### C3: Assumptions & Risk (5 pts)
- C3.1 Critical Assumptions (2)
- C3.2 Risk Assessment Matrix (2)
- C3.3 Mitigation & Contingency (1)

### C4: Impact Measurement (3 pts)
- C4.1 SMART Indicators (2)
- C4.2 Measurement Feasibility (1)

### C5: Adaptive Management (2 pts)
- C5.1 Monitoring & Feedback Loops (1)
- C5.2 Learning & Knowledge Management (1)

---

## PART D — SROI Assessment (25 pts)

### D1: Forecast SROI Model (10 pts)
- D1.1 Input Identification & Valuation (3)
- D1.2 Outcome Valuation & Financial Proxy (4)
- D1.3 Impact Adjustment: DW × AT × DP × Dropoff (3)

### D2: SROI Calculation & Sensitivity (8 pts)
- D2.1 NPV + Discount Rate (4)
- D2.2 Sensitivity 3 Scenarios (2)
- D2.3 Transparency & Documentation (2)

### D3: Evaluative SROI Plan (7 pts)
- D3.1 Baseline & Counterfactual (3)
- D3.2 Stakeholder Engagement (2)
- D3.3 Reporting & Learning (2)

## Minimum Thresholds (บังคับ)

```
A ≥ 9  AND  B ≥ 8  AND  C ≥ 12  AND  D ≥ 12
```

ไม่ผ่าน threshold ใด → FAIL + ระบุ dimension ที่ต้องแก้

## Score Interpretation (Total 100)

| Total | Grade | Level | Recommendation |
|:-----:|:-----:|-------|----------------|
| 85–100 | A+/A | Exceptional/Excellent | STRONGLY FUND |
| 64–84 | B+/B | Very Good/Good | FUND |
| 44–63 | C+/C | Fair/Marginal | CONDITIONAL |
| < 44 | F | Poor | DO NOT FUND |

## Evidence Quality Tags (ใช้ใน scorecard)

- ✅ **Strong**: Direct evidence, document, data
- ⚠️ **Partial**: Implied or incomplete
- ❌ **Missing**: No evidence found
- ❓ **Unclear**: Ambiguous

Rule: ทุกคะแนนต้องมี tag หนึ่งใน 4 แบบ
