## TRL Gate (Technology Readiness Level)

| TRL | Stage | บพข. Accept? |
|:---:|-------|:------------:|
| 1 | Basic research | ❌ (ใช้ทุน วช. แทน) |
| 2 | Technology concept | ❌ |
| 3 | Experimental proof of concept | ⚠️ (Biotech เท่านั้น) |
| 4 | **Technology validated in lab** | ✅ **Minimum gate** |
| 5 | Technology validated in relevant env | ✅ |
| 6 | Technology demonstrated in relevant env | ✅ (Smart Mfg min) |
| 7 | System prototype in operational env | ✅✅ |
| 8 | System complete and qualified | ✅✅ |
| 9 | Actual system proven in operational env | ✅✅ (commercialization ready) |

**Rule:** ต้องระบุ current TRL + target TRL ใน proposal

## Sector Detail: 1. Precision Agriculture & Smart Farming

### Eligible Innovations
- IoT sensors สำหรับ soil/climate monitoring
- AI/ML สำหรับ crop prediction, disease detection
- Smart irrigation systems
- Agricultural drones & imaging
- Farm management platforms
- Post-harvest technology

### Key Stakeholders
- เกษตรกร (primary beneficiary)
- สหกรณ์การเกษตร
- กระทรวงเกษตรและสหกรณ์
- บริษัท agritech
- มหาวิทยาลัยเกษตร

### Typical KPIs
- ผลผลิตต่อไร่เพิ่ม ≥ 15%
- ต้นทุนลด ≥ 20%
- จำนวนเกษตรกรที่ adopt ≥ 100 ราย
- รายได้เกษตรกรเพิ่ม ≥ 12,000 THB/ครัวเรือน/ปี

## Sector Detail: 2. Biopharmaceuticals & Medical Devices

### Eligible Innovations
- Vaccines (novel platforms)
- Biosimilars / Biologics
- Medical imaging devices
- Point-of-care diagnostics
- Digital therapeutics
- AI for medical imaging/pathology

### Regulatory Gates
- อย. approval pathway ต้องชัดเจน
- GMP/GLP compliance plan
- Clinical trial strategy (Phase I–III)
- IRB approval required for human subjects

### Typical KPIs
- Patient reached
- DALY averted
- ลดค่ารักษาพยาบาล ≥ 10%
- Export ready (TFDA → US FDA / EMA)

## Sector Detail: 3. Clean Energy & Carbon Neutrality

### Eligible Innovations
- Solar PV (perovskite, tandem, flexible)
- Energy storage (Li-ion, flow battery, hydrogen)
- Carbon capture (DAC, CCS, BECCS)
- Smart grid & demand response
- EV charging infrastructure
- Biofuels & SAF

### Key Metrics
- CO₂ reduction (tCO₂e/yr)
- Levelized Cost of Energy (LCOE)
- Grid contribution (MW installed)
- Carbon credit eligibility (TGO/Verra/Gold Standard)

### Thai Carbon Neutrality Goals
- Carbon Neutrality by 2050
- Net Zero by 2065
- Thailand's NDC: -30% by 2030

## Sector Detail: 4. Smart Manufacturing

### Eligible Innovations
- Industrial IoT & predictive maintenance
- Digital twin for factory optimization
- Collaborative robotics (cobots)
- Additive manufacturing (3D printing metal)
- Industry 4.0 platforms
- Cybersecurity for OT

- Automotive (EV supply chain)
- Electronics (semiconductor, PCB)
- Food & Beverage
- Chemical & Petrochemical
- Textile (technical textiles)

## Sector Detail: 5. Food Innovation & BCG

### Eligible Innovations
- Alternative protein (plant-based, cultivated meat, insect)
- Functional food & nutraceuticals
- Food safety (blockchain traceability, rapid testing)
- Novel food processing (HPP, PEF, cold plasma)
- Upcycled food (food waste to product)
- Probiotics & fermentation

### Certifications
- Halal / Kosher (export ready)
- Novel Food regulatory pathway
- BCG certification
- Sustainability labels (carbon neutral, water neutral)

## Eligibility Checklist

ก่อนให้คะแนน ตรวจ:

- [ ] Sector ตรงกับ 8 target (หรือ cross-cutting)?
- [ ] ววน. Platform ระบุชัดเจน (P1–P5)?
- [ ] Frontier Technology ระบุ ≥ 1?
- [ ] Current TRL ≥ gate ของ sector?
- [ ] Regulatory pathway clear (if applicable)?
- [ ] Industry partner (MOU) — especially for commercialization?

**FAIL:** ถ้า sector ไม่อยู่ใน 8 target และไม่มี ววน. alignment → ส่งไป วช. หรือ PMU อื่น
